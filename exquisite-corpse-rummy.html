Assemble the table

Build monsters from a skull, optional claws, and a foot — all of one suit (wild fills any gap, but wild parts can be stolen). Empty your hand to trigger the last turn; score +1 per card you played into a monster and −1 per card left in your hand.

Players:
— DRAW
Rules & how to play
Your turn: Draw → Build / Steal → Discard.
Draw — click the LAB deck, or click any card in the graveyard. The top graveyard card is free. Digging deeper is only allowed if that card can complete a monster this turn, and you must also sweep every card sitting on top of it into your hand.
Build — select cards in your hand and press Build monster. A monster is exactly one skull + one foot, with any number of claws, all sharing a suit. Wild parts can fill a missing suit. Build as many as you like.
Steal — switch to Steal mode and click a glowing wild part inside any monster, then click a matching real card (same suit + same part) from your hand to swap it in. The wild returns to your hand and can be rebuilt this turn.
Discard — end your turn by sending one card to the graveyard. If your hand is empty you may end the turn instead.
Round end — emptying your hand triggers the last turn; everyone else gets one more turn, then scores are tallied.
